===========================VIM Tips===============================


Add , at end line
:<>s/\n/,\r/g

Remove , at end line
:<>s/,\n/\r/g

Empty line remove(space+TAB) 
:%g/^\s*$/d
:%g/^[ ^I]*$/d

Remove white space at end line
:<>s/\s\+$//g
:<>s/[ ^I]*$//g

Remove white space at the middle of sentence
:<>s/\s//g
:<>s/[ ^I]//g


Replace Nth word at the line
:%s/\(\zsPATTERN.\{-}\)\{N}/REPLACE/

Replace second '_' to ',' at line
:%s/\(\zs_.\{-}\)\{2}/,/g
